Illusions - our daily life VR.

Alonso Ahmetaj
JS Fullstack Web Dev - All things Tech/Writer, Design, Photography passionate.

These days is easy and almost normal to feel lost into some kind of void or loop that is difficult to break through despite the awareness of it. We know this! Yet we don’t apply that knowledge to the circumstances, to the life style and environment around us. We can see this in business, entrepreneurship, politics — Oh yes politics ha.. ha… ehm…in every area that has to do with a human being!

Applied knowledge if that makes sense should be the focus of education these days. Just killing our memories with information that we might not use at all is not the ideal situation you would want to live in. Every one wants to be successful in business, entrepreneurship, life etc who doesn’t but then we do really need to ask ourselves with brutal honesty why is not working?

It is an illusion! The problem lies on the brain’s ability to create a virtual reality that in reality doesn’t coincide with the real world or whatever path one is pursuing. The fact is that everyone knows things! Every one knows what is right or wrong! But we know it with our brains, inside our virtual reality. We lack the need to know it with our actions!
